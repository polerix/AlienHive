# 🌊 XENO HIVE | The Aqua Protocol 🌊

> *Survival in the dark green water of the digital deep.* 🫧

---

🐚 **Xeno Hive** is a fluid survival experience. You inhabit the role of a Specialist Marine navigating a multi-level colonial complex. Your mission: contain the rising digital tide of the Xenomorph threat.

---

## 🌊 Game Features (Level 2: The Complex) 🌊

- **🫧 Multi-Level Flow** — Navigate through a 3-floor procedural complex.
- **🐚 Auto-Tiled Architecture** — Seamlessly rendered rooms, labs, and medical bays with intelligent wall alignment.
- **🫧 Interactive Systems** — Operate wall switches and control panels to manage doors and facility state.
- **🐚 Full Life Cycle** — Witness the relentless progression from Egg to Facehugger, Chestburster, and adult Xeno.
- **🫧 Specialized Roles** — Interact with Colonists, Corpos, and Synths, each with unique behaviors and survival instincts.

---

## 🛠️ Controls & Interaction 🛠️

- **🌊 Movement:** `WASD` or **Arrow Keys** to navigate the complex.
- **🫧 Combat:** **Left-Click** to fire your Pulse Rifle.
- **🐚 Interaction:** Press `E` to operate switches, panels, and facility controls.
- **🌊 Camera:** The view fluidly follows your Marine as you dive deeper.
- **🫧 Zoom:** Use the **Mouse Wheel** to adjust your tactical perspective.
- **🐚 Speed:** `F1` / `F2` to adjust the current of time.

---

## 🧬 The Xeno Life Cycle 🧬

1. **The Egg:** Hatches a Facehugger when non-Xeno life is detected nearby.
2. **The Facehugger:** High-speed pursuit; infects hosts on contact.
3. **The Infection:** Progresses within the host (visible status bar).
4. **The Chestburster:** Erupts from the host, searching for safety to mature.
5. **The Drone:** The fully matured hunter, ready to expand the hive.

---

## 🫧 Colonial Roles 🫧

- **Marines:** Tactical combat specialists. Never panic. Armed with Pulse Rifles.
- **Colonists:** High panic threshold. Attempt to escape and report threats.
- **Corpos:** Tasked with egg retrieval. Can unlock and operate facility panels.
- **Synths:** Unarmed but vital. Can repair ruined panels and medical vats.

---

## 🗺️ Facility Logic 🗺️

Control panels and wall switches are the nervous system of the complex. 
- **Switches:** Open nearby doors.
- **Panels:** Can be open, bolted, or covered.
- **Damage:** Combat can ruin panels; only Synths or Corpos can restore flow.
